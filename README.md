
# 🥛 Dairy Goods Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI Business Intelligence Dashboard** built to analyze dairy sales, inventory performance, customer demand, and farm operations. The dashboard transforms raw business data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations, helping stakeholders monitor KPIs, identify trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

#  Project Overview

The **Dairy Goods Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed using **Power BI**. It provides a comprehensive analysis of dairy business operations from **2019–2022**, covering sales performance, inventory management, farm efficiency, and customer insights.

The dashboard enables users to:

- Monitor key business KPIs.
- Analyze product and brand performance.
- Track inventory health and reorder requirements.
- Evaluate farm productivity.
- Generate actionable business insights.

---

#  Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development and interactive visualization.
- 🔄 **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing.
- 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Created calculated measures, KPIs, and business metrics.
- 🗂 **Data Modeling** – Established relationships between multiple tables for efficient filtering and reporting.
- 📈 **Power BI Visualizations** – KPI Cards, Line Charts, Bar Charts, Column Charts, Treemap, Donut Chart, Maps, Matrix, Tables, and Slicers.
- 📁 **File Format** – .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.

---

#  Data Source

**Dataset:** Dairy Goods Sales Dataset

The dataset taken fron Kaggle and it contains information related to dairy business operations, including:

- Product Details
- Brand Information
- Sales Transactions
- Farm Locations
- Customer Locations
- Inventory Levels
- Shelf Life
- Stock Quantity
- Reorder Quantity
- Sales Channels
- Revenue & Pricing

The dashboard analyzes:

- 📦 **10 Products**
- 🏢 **11 Brands**
- 📍 **15 Farm Locations**
- 📅 **2019–2022 Sales Data**

#  Dashboard Features & Highlights

##  Business Problem

Managing dairy business operations requires monitoring multiple business areas simultaneously, including sales, inventory, products, customer demand, and farm performance. Analyzing these metrics manually makes it difficult to identify trends, monitor inventory, and make informed business decisions.

---

##  Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard was designed to:

- Monitor overall business performance through interactive KPIs.
- Analyze revenue trends and product performance.
- Evaluate inventory health and stock availability.
- Compare farm productivity across different locations.
- Generate business insights and recommendations.

---

###  Page 5 – Business Insights & Recommendations

Summarizes key findings and provides actionable recommendations.

#### Key Insights

- Curd generated the highest revenue.
- Retail contributed the highest revenue among all sales channels.
- 97.68% of inventory remained healthy.
- Chandigarh generated the highest revenue.
- Revenue remained stable throughout the analysis period.

#### Business Recommendations

- Increase production of high-demand products.
- Improve inventory planning using reorder quantity analysis.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing customer locations.
- Improve farm productivity by increasing revenue per cow.
- Strengthen retail sales while expanding online sales.
